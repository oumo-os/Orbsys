- 100 members organized into 5 departments
- Each department paralyzed, waiting for others

**The PAAS Solution:**
- 50 members across 20 Circles
- Participating in 100+ concurrent STFs
- Coordination overhead: 15-25% of work time
- Zero bottlenecks
- Continuous innovation

**The Result:** PAAS organizations achieve 3-5x the effective output per member compared to traditional structures.
